## Common Tasks

### Task 1: Create a New Invoice
1. Go to **Invoices** tab
2. Click "+" button (top left)
3. Select student
4. Choose fee type (Monthly, One-time, Package, Per-Class, Custom)
5. Enter amount and due date
6. Click "Generate Invoice"

### Task 2: Record a Payment
1. Go to **Invoices** or **To Pay** tab
2. Find the student
3. Click **"Record Payment"** (green button)
4. Enter:
   - **Date** of payment
   - **Amount** paid
   - **Method**: 
     - ğŸ’µ **Cash**
     - ğŸ“± **PhonePe**
     - ğŸ¦ **Online** (Bank Transfer)
     - ğŸ’³ **Card**
     - ğŸª **Bank**
     - âœ“ **Other**
   - **Note** (optional) - e.g., "Part payment of 5000"
5. Click "Record Payment"

### Task 3: Contact Parent About Pending Payment
1. Go to **To Pay** tab
2. Find the student
3. Click **"WhatsApp Parent"** (green button)
   - WhatsApp opens automatically
   - Send a friendly reminder about payment
4. Once paid, click **"Log Payment"** to record it

### Task 4: Follow Up on Overdue Payments
1. Go to **Overdue** tab
2. See list of students sorted by **days overdue**
3. Click **"ğŸ“± WhatsApp Now"** to contact parent immediately
4. Once payment received, click **"Log Payment"**
5. Payment is recorded with current date and method

### Task 5: Download Payment Report
1. Click **"Download Excel Report"** (top right, download icon)
2. Open the Excel file - it has 4 sheets:
   - **Fee Summary**: All invoices and balances
   - **Payment Log**: Every payment made (with method!)
   - **Payment Methods**: How much cash vs PhonePe vs online
   - **Summary Stats**: Quick overview numbers
3. Use for:
   - Reconciling with bank account
   - Counting cash on hand
   - Weekly/monthly reports

## Need Help?

**WhatsApp button not working?**
- Make sure parent phone is saved in student profile
- Must be valid Indian phone number (10 digits)

**Payment method not showing?**
- Check that you selected a method when recording payment
- Refresh page to see updated

**Excel not downloading?**
- Check browser download settings
- Make sure you have at least one fee/payment
- Try clearing browser cache

**Numbers don't match?**
- Check filters in Invoices view
- Ensure all payments are recorded with correct methods
- Review Payment Log sheet in Excel
